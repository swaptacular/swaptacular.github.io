To try Swaptacular, it is best to have two people — or at least two
devices (for example, a laptop and a smartphone). This allows you to
**issue your own currency** on one device and **create a digital
wallet** on the other. Using two separate devices also makes it easy
to scan QR codes between them.


## Issue your own currency

It is best to use your desktop or laptop computer for this. Make sure
your computer has a camera, as you will need it to scan QR codes.

<blockquote>
  👉 <a href="{{ site.app_demo.debtors_webapp }}" target="_blank"> The
  “My Currency” app</a> will guide you through the process of creating
  your own digital currency. At the end of the process, a QR code
  representing your currency — your “digital coin” — will be
  generated.
</blockquote>

Once you have created your currency, you will be able to issue any
amount of it to your digital wallet.

**Note:** When setting up your currency, it is strongly recommended to
declare a fixed exchange rate between your currency and a well-known
currency such as the US dollar.

## Create a digital wallet

It is best to use your smartphone for this.

<blockquote>
  👉 <a href="{{ site.app_demo.creditors_webapp }}" target="_blank">
  The “My wallet” app</a> allows you to hold and use other people’s
  currencies, including your own. Note that you will need to
  create a separate user account for this app (you can use the same
  email address).
</blockquote>

During setup, the app will ask you for a “digital coin” QR code. You
can use the one generated for your own currency, or you can scan the
digital coin for *this demo currency*:

![Example digital coin](/images/example_digital_coin.png)

Once the app has the QR code for the currency you want to use, you
will be able to request payments in that currency and process payment
requests from other people.

**Note:** When you want to use a new currency, and the issuer has
declared a fixed exchange rate between that currency and a well-known
currency, like the US dollar, you will be asked to approve this
*currency peg*.

To issue your own currency to your wallet:

1. Scan the digital coin QR code for your own currency.
2. Create a payment request for the desired amount.
3. Scan the payment request QR code **with the “My currency” app** and
   approve the payment.


## Automated currency exchanges

Setting up automated currency exchanges manually for testing can be a
bit involved.

You would need to create multiple currencies and wallets (using
different user accounts), distribute funds between them, and configure
exchange policies. You would also need to wait a day or two, as the
matching system runs overnight.

In practice, you do not need to do this. As more participants join
your local trading community, opportunities for exchange naturally
emerge — and are handled automatically by the system.
